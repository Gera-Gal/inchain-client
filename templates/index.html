    About this project

    In The Chain
    Self-paced crypto learning platform

    The pandemic represents a milestone in the lives of many people, in my case, it caused a series of isolated elements in my past to line up, leading me to the crypto universe.

    With this project I seek to make available to anyone who needs it a set of resources to learn about blockchain and go from "what in the world is that" to "oh, blockchain, my old and dearest friend".

    At some point in the development of this platform, rankings of crypto assets, influential people, videos and readings will be available so that you can find your favorite crypto project or crypto projects.
